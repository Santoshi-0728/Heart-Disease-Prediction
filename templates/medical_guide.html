Medical Input Field Guide

1. Education Level

  • 1 - Less than High School
  • 2 - High School Graduate
  • 3 - Some College or Vocational Training
  • 4 - College Graduate

2. Current Smoker

  • Yes - You currently smoke.
  • No - You don’t currently smoke.

3. Cigarettes Per Day

  • Enter number of cigarettes you smoke daily. High values increase risk.

4. BP Medication

  • Yes - You are on blood pressure medication.
  • No - You are not on any BP medication.

5. History of Stroke

  • Yes - You had a stroke before.
  • No - No history of stroke.

6. Hypertension

  • Yes - You have high blood pressure (usually > 140/90 mm Hg).
  • No - Normal blood pressure.

7. Diabetes

  • Yes - You have diabetes (usually glucose > 126 mg/dL fasting).
  • No - You don’t have diabetes.

8. Total Cholesterol

  • Below 200 mg/dL - Desirable
  • 200–239 mg/dL - Borderline High
  • 240 mg/dL and above - High

9. Systolic & Diastolic Blood Pressure

  • Normal: 120/80 mm Hg
  • High: 140/90 mm Hg and above

10. BMI (Body Mass Index)

  • Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: 30+

11. Heart Rate

  • Normal Resting: 60–100 bpm
  • High Risk: Above 100 bpm consistently

12. Glucose

  • Normal: Less than 100 mg/dL (fasting)
  • Pre-diabetes: 100–125 mg/dL
  • Diabetes: 126+ mg/dL
